Welcome to St. Anne's

A Second Home Where Young Hearts Blossom

Started in 1977 by the Sisters of St. Anne, Bangalore, with just 30 students in a rented house, our institution has grown — through the untiring efforts of management, staff, parents and well-wishers — into a beacon of learning for the rural children of Sira and its surrounding areas.

  • Pupils of all religious denominations admitted without distinction of faith
  • No selection criteria — serving every strata of society through education
  • Upgraded to High School in 1985 and PU College in 2015

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Our Mission

To educate and empower students through academic excellence, spiritual formation and value-based learning — inspiring them to serve God and society with love and integrity as responsible citizens.
