Our Story

About St. Anne's

Run by St. Anne's Educational Society, Bangalore — a beacon of academic excellence, discipline and values since 1977.

Our Heritage

A Legacy of Learning & Values

Through the untiring efforts of management, staff, parents and well-wishers, the institution has grown steadily in strength, discipline and academic excellence.

1977

The Beginning

Started on 15th June 1977 by Sr. Presentation in a rented house, with just 30 students in the first batch. Sr. Rosamma served as the first Headmistress.

1985

Upgraded to High School

On 16th July 1985, recognising the rural community's growing educational needs, the school was upgraded to a High School — opening new doors of knowledge with Sr. Benigna as first Headmistress.

2015

St. Anne's PU College

A new era began on 15th June 2015 with the opening of the PU College, offering combinations in PCMB, PCMC & EBAC.

Today

49 Years Strong

More than 40 batches of SSLC students have passed out meritoriously with cent-percent results, securing ranks at Taluk, District and State levels.

Our Vision
