Our Story of Faith, Growth & Service

From a rented house with 30 students in 1977 to a thriving institution shaping generations — discover the journey of St. Anne's School, Sira.

History of the Institution

Nearly Five Decades of Untiring Effort

St. Anne's School, Sira — run by the management of St. Anne's Educational Society, Bangalore — was started on 15th June 1977 by Sr. Presentation, the then Superior General of the Sisters of St. Anne, Bangalore. Sr. Agnes was the first Superior of the community, and Sr. Rosamma was appointed the first Head Mistress, along with Sisters Rosy I and Rosaline Antony, who started the school at Sira.

Thirty students were enrolled in the first batch, in a rented house. The pioneers faced many hurdles to bring up this institution to its present level. Through the untiring efforts of the management, staff, parents and well-wishers, the institution gradually grew in strength, discipline and academic excellence.

Recognising the increasing educational needs of rural children, the school was upgraded to High School on 16th July 1985, with Sr. Benigna as the first Headmistress — a historic movement that opened new doors of knowledge. A new era began with the opening of St. Anne's PU College on 15th June 2015, with combinations of PCMB, PCMC and EBAC.

Objective of the Institution

Faithful to "Love and Service"

This school remains faithful to the objective and motto of our Educational Society — "Love and Service" to humanity — by imparting knowledge to the children of the locality and its nearby areas. Pupils of all religious denominations are admitted without any difference of faith.

We admit students without any selection criteria, as our main aim is to serve the lower strata of society and to transform it through education. We are proud to be the first English medium school in Sira to run with departmental permission.

Life at St. Anne's

A Beautiful Journey of Transformation

Life at St. Anne's School, Sira is like a beautiful journey filled with joy in learning, love for discipline and respect for values — woven with unforgettable memories. The school stands as a second home where students are lovingly nurtured in an atmosphere of care, unity and inspiration.

Every day begins with prayer and positive thoughts, filling the campus with peace, hope and enthusiasm. The classrooms echo with curiosity and learning, while the playground blooms with energy, teamwork and laughter. Here, young hearts blossom with knowledge, faith, values and dreams.

A school is where a child's confidence is built, abilities are discovered, attitudes are shaped and personality is developed.

The memories created within its walls remain forever cherished in the hearts of students, teachers and parents.
